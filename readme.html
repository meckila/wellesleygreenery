Color Guidlines

In terms of color guidelines, it was important to use colors related to the website theme, vegetation. This resulted in a monochromatic color scheme that would prevent a sensory overload for the user. Green also has a lower color sensitivity level than yellows or reds. In order to place highlight certain features (icons, links, buttons) in brighter fonts. A desaturated, white background also allowed for contrast against dominant, readable text.

Visual Variables

For visual variables, size and orientation were most important for content organization. The back button is located in the top left corner, while the title is at the top of the page. In addition, button sizes were determined by hierarchy. For example, the title of the subsection under 'Greenhouses' (i.e. Greenhouse Rooms, Favorite Flora) were smaller than their parent section ('Greenhouses'). Lighter colors were used to indicate links to other pages as well.
